What is Marble?

Marble is a web and mobile SaaS project made for landlords wanting a more simplified rental management experience. Focused on automating many of the processes many older project management companies continue to do manually, a lot of the property life-cycle has been done programatically to reduce the overhead needed to find a tenant or keep a property maintained.


As a software engineer part of the engineering team, my focus was on improving user experience and reducing manual workload from operations, designed and implemented multiple features to alleviate any friction and obstacles between the customer and product.


What I worked on

Projects

Improved Onboarding Process for new landlords:

- Guiding new users through the neccessary steps required to properly use the product.

- Adding their first property and beginning their first workflow to experience and learn how the product functions.

- Reduced questions and misunderstandings from new users and increased property creation by 20%.


Lease Creation and Signing Flow for newly accepted applications

- A major contributor from designing the spec and implementation of the feature/flow.

- Utlized Slate.js, an open source rich text editor, to generate a lease for landlords based on the information provided for the property and applicant, allowing conditional statements and other phrases to be displayed or hidden.

- Integrated Docusign API and Webhook services to automatically create and monitor the signing process for both the landlord and tenants.

- Removed the requirement for manual operations from the Marble team and reduced the leasing and tenant onboarding process time by 3-5 days.


Pivotal Product Changes to automate the property rental lifecycle,

- Revamped the product from a toolbox made for properties property to a guided and seamless experience for landlords taking them through the property rental life cycle, all from leasing to maintenance.

- A major contributor from designing the spec and implementation of the feature/flow.

- Utlized Temporal.js to enable frequent checks and operations for each specific proccess the user is in.


Shippo Integration for Smart Lock Orders

- Integrated Shippo to allow shippment creation and instant ordering to distributors.

- Shipping labels automatically created and sent to distrinbutors to allow faster shipment and delivery times.

- Implemented shipment tracking and management for landlords, allowing them to keep track of the shipment as well as cancel locks if needed.
